Our Mission

At WHPM, our goal is to spread the word of God and help empower individuals to lead victorious lives through faith in Jesus Christ. We are committed to uplifting our community with the teachings of the Bible. Our ministry aims to build a strong Christian foundation, enabling all who join us to grow spiritually and contribute to the advancement of God's Kingdom.
